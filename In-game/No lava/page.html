After dying in lava I can’t respawn

this is a bug the only way to respawn is to log into the server on a computer, respawn and then log back in on your quest

​

alternatively you could make sure this gamerule is enabled, this will not give you the option to respawn and you will auto respawn immediately - /gamerule doImmediateRespawn true We have tested the command to see if enabling it after you have died fixes the issue it doesn't

​

another option is to delete your player data file, however deleting this will delete everything about your player, including ender chests, bed spawn and your location this file is located in world/playerdata/‘youruuid’ to find your uuid just join the server and it will be displayed in the console/ your Minecraft log file. Alternatively you can find it by searching for your username on https://namemc.com/

​

or if you really know what you're doing you could edit your player.dat file manually
